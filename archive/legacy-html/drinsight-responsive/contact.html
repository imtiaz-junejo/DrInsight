Common Queries

Frequently Asked Contact Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about reaching our team

How quickly will I receive a response?

▾
Email enquiries are typically answered within 2–4 hours during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8AM–8PM EST). WhatsApp messages are answered within 1 hour. Phone calls are answered immediately during office hours. After-hours messages receive a response on the next business day.

Can I contact you in a language other than English?

▾
Yes — our support team speaks English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, and Mandarin. Please mention your preferred language in your message and we will assign a representative who speaks your language.

How do I cancel or reschedule a consultation?

▾
You can cancel or reschedule via your patient portal, by emailing drinsightofficial@gmail.com with your booking reference, or by calling our helpline. Free rescheduling is available up to 2 hours before your appointment. Full refunds are issued for cancellations made 24+ hours in advance.

I'm a doctor — how do I join DrInsight's panel?

▾
We welcome board-certified physicians to join our platform. Please email drinsightofficial@gmail.com with your CV, medical licence, board certification details, and a brief statement of interest. Our medical director reviews all applications within 5 business days.

How do I report a technical issue with the platform?

▾
Select "Technical Issue" in the contact form above and describe the problem in detail — include your browser, device, and a screenshot if possible. Our technical team typically resolves reported issues within 24 hours. For urgent issues during a live consultation, call our helpline immediately.

Do you offer media interviews or expert quotes?

▾
Yes — Dr. Javed Kumbhar and our specialist team are available for media interviews, expert quotes, and health commentary. Please contact drinsightofficial@gmail.com with your publication details, deadline, and topic. We typically respond to media enquiries within 4 hours.
