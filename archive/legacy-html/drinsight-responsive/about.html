A Message from Our Founder
"

Building a Platform Where Every Patient Deserves the Truth

When I began my medical career over a decade ago, I was struck by one consistent challenge: patients were leaving consultations confused, overwhelmed, or misinformed — not because their doctors lacked expertise, but because the healthcare system lacked accessibility and clarity.

I founded DrInsight in 2022 with a simple conviction — that accurate, evidence-based medical information should be a right, not a privilege. Every person, regardless of their location, income, or background, deserves access to the kind of trusted guidance that only a good doctor can provide.

What started as a small team of passionate physicians has grown into a platform trusted by over 10,000 patients across 50 countries. Our doctors don't just answer questions — they empower patients to make confident, informed decisions about their health. Every article is reviewed. Every tool is validated. Every consultation is held to the highest clinical standard.

We are not just a website. We are a movement toward a healthier, better-informed world. And we are only just beginning.

✚
Dr. Javed Kumbhar MBBS, RMP — Founder & Board of Director, DrInsight 📍 Karachi, Pakistan  |  🌐 drinsight.org

Our Standards

Editorial Guidelines & Medical Review Process

Every piece of content on DrInsight passes through a rigorous multi-step medical review process before it reaches you. We follow the highest journalistic and clinical standards.

Our editorial team includes medical writers, licensed physicians, and specialty reviewers who collaborate to ensure accuracy, clarity, and patient safety.

1

Research & Drafting

Experienced medical writers research the topic using peer-reviewed sources and clinical guidelines only.

2

Specialist Review

A board-certified specialist in the relevant field reviews all clinical claims and recommendations.

3

Editorial Approval

Our senior editor reviews for clarity, tone, and patient-appropriateness before publication.

4

Quarterly Updates

All content is reviewed every 3–6 months and updated when new evidence or guidelines are published.
